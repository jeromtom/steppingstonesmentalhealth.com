My Energy Equation Status

It is within your power to improve your energy and live each day in peace, joy and unlimited fulfillment. The secret is to begin replacing unproductive habits with a way of life that is healthy in mind, body and spirit.

Change will require practice. To monitor your progress along the way, use the Energy Equation Self Assessment Test. It guides you to reflect on ten critical areas of your life.

Begin by establishing your baseline, selecting the statement that best describes your habits. Come back to the Energy Equation Self Assessment as often as you like to retest and check your progress. You will feel your energy improve and your spirits soar as your scores change.
